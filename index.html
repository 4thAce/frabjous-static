Moving over

Sat Dec 29 10:11:49 2007 [permalink]
I am joining the Web 2.0 world now and relaunching this writing blog as http://frabjoustimes.magahiz.com, a Ruby on Rails site using the Typo blogging platform. Although I had a good time tweaking my little Python-based posting script, this will make it easier to control the layout of the pages and to add new posts from anywhere.
If your browser supports Javascript, you will be redirected to the new site in ten seconds. You will probably want to update your bookmarks and feed subscriptions.
